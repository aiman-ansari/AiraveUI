Typography

.h1 through .h6 classes are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but cannot use the associated HTML element.

H2 Paragraph text

H3 Paragraph text

You can also use gray color in text by adding .gray-text into html.

This is gray text.

This is gray text.

Center Text

Center your text by simply adding .flex-align-center

This is Center text

                    
<h2 class="flex-align-center">This is Center text</h2>
